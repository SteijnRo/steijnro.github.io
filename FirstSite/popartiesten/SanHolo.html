San Holo

and his career

San Holo is a Future Bass music producer from the netherlands. He likes
to remix songs from all kinds of games, or just other people's songs.
He does this in such a good way that he has created a massive following
of at least 100 million.

Not only does he remix songs, but he makes them too. He created hits like
"light" or "we rise". Both of these songs have a combind total of views of
105 million views, light having 'only' 15 million views* and "we rise" having
90 million views.* He has more songs than just these two, of course, but these
are the songs I personally like the most.

*both videos from Trap Nation, not all the big videos on youtube.
