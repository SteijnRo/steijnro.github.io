Veorra

Rise to fame and the fall back down

The rise ...

Veorra was a music producer who make trap/edm songs for fun with his accomplice singer.
They released 4 albums called Gold, Saphire, Ruby and Emerald. they grew in popularity
quickly cause of their new sounding high quality music, they gained over 100K subscribers
on youtube with just those 4 albums. They got over 2 million views on some songs thanks to
trap nation and other big youtube music outlets.

... and the fall.

Now it wasn't all rainbows and sunshine for the two,
after some time they agreed to split ways and they didnt touch the channel since.
The sadness from the fans was a sight to behold, but seeing as their favorite music producers
split up it is very understandable. Till this day the comment section is still filled with fans
wondering when the group will return, but the group coming back together is highly unlikely.

Soooo... what now?

Both the producer as the singer have gone their seperate ways in music producing, the producer
is now called "Neovaii", but the singer is very hard to find, she atill sings in EDM music,
but locating her is a real job now.
