Avicii's career and his end

Avicii was a great dance/edm artist who found happyness in his music.
He was a person who likes to be by himself producing or listening to all
kinds of music. He got famous by creating all kinds of upbeat decently
fast-paced songs.

But the fame became too much for him and commit suicide. He was found
dead by a Sultan's family member on their ground which he was residing
on. It took the world by storm and it created tribute videos all over
the internet.
